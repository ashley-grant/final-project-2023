Book Fair Extraviganza

October 15-17, 2023

City Convention Center, 123 Main Street, Anytown

Immerse yourself in the world of books at our community book fair! We are celebrating literature and fostering a love for reading!

About

This is a wide community event witht the purpose of promoting literacy, supporting local authors, and fostering a love for reading. Here is important Information for Attendees. Parking will be help in the south parking lot free of cost. There will be an admission fee of $15 at the door. In order to attend you must be 18+ or have a legal gaurdian if younger.

Schedule

There will be many sessions and activities. Held in Remenal Hall will be author readings. There will be panel discussions in the offices downstairs. The workshops will be held in the commons.
